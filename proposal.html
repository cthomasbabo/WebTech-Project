My Girls Abroad

A website dedicated to providing college students a guide with popular abroad destinations and reccomendations.

What is My Girls Abroad about?

My Girls Abroad is a tool to maximize your abroad experience. Whether you are a student, travelling for work, or moving abroad, my goal is to share recommendations and experiences from fellow travelers.

What is the objective of this website?

The goal is to create a site rich with content for popular abroad destinations tailored primarily to a female audience. Content will cover a range of topics including: dining, travel, events, fashion, lifestyle, night-life, tips, etc.
